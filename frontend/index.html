Your Data, Your Control

DermAI is committed to protecting your sensitive health data. We follow industry best practices to ensure your privacy.

📋

Data We Collect

  • Account Info — Name, email, and skin tone preference
  • Skin Images — Photos you upload for AI analysis (processed in-memory, not stored permanently on our servers)
  • Symptom Text — Descriptions you type for text-based analysis
  • Scan History — Condition names, confidence scores, and timestamps (stored locally in your browser)
  • Location Data — Only when you explicitly use "Find Dermatologist" — never tracked in background
  • Usage Analytics — Anonymous scan counts and region data via Firebase Analytics (if configured)
⚙️

How We Use Your Data

  • Images are sent to Google Gemini AI for real-time analysis and are not retained after processing
  • Your scan history is stored locally in your browser (localStorage) — we do not upload it to any cloud database
  • Location data is used only once per request to find nearby dermatologists — it is never stored or tracked
  • Anonymous analytics help us improve the platform — no personal health data is included
  • We never sell, share, or monetize your personal or health data
🔒

Data Storage & Security

  • All API communication uses HTTPS encryption in transit
  • Authentication tokens are JWT-signed and expire after 7 days
  • Passwords are hashed with bcrypt (10 salt rounds) — never stored in plain text
  • Scan history, theme preferences, and API keys are stored in your browser's localStorage
  • Rate limiting protects against abuse (100 requests per 15 minutes)

Your Rights

You have full control over your data. Use the actions below at any time:

📥
Download My Data
Export all your locally stored data (scan history, settings, preferences) as a JSON file.
🗑️
Delete My Data
Permanently erase all your local data including scan history, saved keys, and preferences. This cannot be undone.
🚫
Revoke Consent
Withdraw your data processing consent. This will sign you out and delete all stored data.
